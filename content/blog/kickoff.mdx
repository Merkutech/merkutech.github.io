## Project Kickoff

We are excited to announce the start of our SUAS 2026 competition journey. This year, our team is focusing on building a robust and reliable unmanned aerial system capable of autonomous mission execution.

### Initial Planning

Our first steps included:

- Reviewing the 2026 competition rules and requirements
- Defining team roles and responsibilities
- Setting up our development environment and toolchain
- Creating a project timeline with key milestones

### Design Considerations

The vehicle design phase involves careful consideration of:

1. **Aerodynamics** — minimizing drag while maximizing payload capacity
2. **Flight endurance** — targeting 45+ minutes of autonomous flight
3. **Payload integration** — ensuring seamless communication between subsystems
4. **Safety systems** — implementing redundant failsafe mechanisms

Stay tuned for more updates as we progress through the design and development phases.
